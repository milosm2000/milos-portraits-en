Services and prices

I draw realistic portraits with a graphite pencil based on the photo you send me.

Once you have decided to order a portrait from me, here is the process:

I need a reference photo of the person's face. The photo must be of good quality because the realism of the drawing depends on it.

Prices and Formats:

- A5 format (14.8 cm x 21 cm), one person - 140 EUR
- A4 format (21 cm x 29.7 cm), one person - 160 EUR
- A3 format (29.7 cm x 42 cm), one person - 180 EUR


- Larger paper sizes or drawings by more than one person can also be arranged, in which case the price will be calculated based on your request.

The production time is a maximum of 4 weeks.

Payment:

A prepayment of 5 EUR is required (I use this money to buy materials if needed, but more importantly this payment serves as proof that you are serious about the order as I put many hours into drawing ).
The prepayment is made as a 5 EUR donation on the Ko-Fi platform (you have officially ordered a portrait from me when you buy me a coffee ðŸ˜Ž), and the rest of the payment is made in cash when we meet.
