Service and Pricing

Greetings, I draw realistic portraits with a graphite pencil based on the photo you send me.

If you have decided to order a portrait from me, here is the process:

I need a reference photo of the persons face. Photo must be of good quality, because the realism of the drawing
depends on it.

Pricing and formats:

- 30×40 cm graphite pencil realistic portrait of one person - 220.00 EUR (200 EUR + 20 EUR advance payment).

- Larger paper formats or drawings of more then one person can also be arranged, and the price for that will
be determined based on details of your request.

Production time is maximally 4-weeks.

I live in 16. district, so based on your location we can arrange the delivery of portrait. 

Payment in advance in the amount of 20 EUR is required. It can be done via Western Union or Ria Transfer.

If you are interested, send me a message with the reference photo via whatsapp/viber (+381 63 7135922) or email
me at mirkovic.milos007(ät)gmail.com and I will contact you soon.
